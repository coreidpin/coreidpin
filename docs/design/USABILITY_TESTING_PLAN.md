---

### **SCENARIO 1: Onboarding Flow (New Users Only) - 15 min**

**Task:** "Imagine you heard about CoreIDPin from a colleague. They said it helps verify your professional identity. Sign up and get your PIN."

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] Completes signup in <3 minutes
- [ ] Understands what a PIN is
- [ ] Adds at least one verification (email/phone/work)
- [ ] Reaches success screen

**Observations to Note:**
- Where do they get stuck?
- Do they choose LinkedIn import or manual entry?
- Do they understand trust score?
- Do they skip optional steps?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- "What did you think when you saw the LinkedIn import option?"
- "Was the trust score clear? What does 25/100 mean to you?"
- "Did you feel safe sharing your information?"
- "On a scale of 1-10, how motivated are you to improve your trust score?"

---

### **SCENARIO 2: Dashboard Navigation (All Users) - 10 min**

**Task:** "You just logged in. Find out how many companies viewed your profile this week."

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] Finds profile views metric in <30 seconds
- [ ] Correctly identifies the number
- [ ] Can explain what the trend arrow means

**Observations:**
- Do they scan the page or read everything?
- Do they use tabs or scroll?
- Do they notice the quick actions?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- "What's the most important information on this page?"
- "What would you do next if you were a recruiter looking at this?"
- "Is there anything missing you'd expect to see?"

---

### **SCENARIO 3: Share PIN (All Users) - 5 min**

**Task:** "You're applying for a job and they ask for your verified professional PIN. Share your PIN with them."

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] Finds share button in <20 seconds
- [ ] Successfully copies PIN or generates QR code
- [ ] Understands what they're sharing

**Observations:**
- Do they use quick action or menu?
- Do they prefer PIN text or QR code?
- Do they hesitate before sharing?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- "What information do you think the recruiter will see when they verify your PIN?"
- "Would you feel comfortable sharing this publicly on LinkedIn?"

---

### **SCENARIO 4: Request Endorsement (All Users) - 5 min**

**Task:** "You want to boost your trust score. Request an endorsement from a colleague."

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] Finds endorsement request feature
- [ ] Understands how to send invitation
- [ ] Successfully composes request

**Observations:**
- Do they understand what an endorsement is?
- Do they personalize the message?
- Do they know who to send it to?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- "How is this different from a LinkedIn recommendation?"
- "Would you actually send this to your colleagues?"

---

### **SCENARIO 5: Mobile Bottom Nav (Mobile Users Only) - 5 min**

**Task:** "Switch between Overview, Alerts, and Profile using the bottom navigation."

**Success Criteria:**
- [ ] Can reach all nav items with one hand
- [ ] Understands icon meanings
- [ ] Notices active state

**Observations:**
- Do they use thumb or index finger?
- Do they accidentally tap wrong item?
- Do they notice badge counts?

**Follow-up Questions:**
- "Is the bottom nav easy to reach?"
- "Are the icons clear or confusing?"
- "Would you prefer a top nav or this bottom nav?"

---

### **Post-Test Questions (5 min)**

1. **Overall Impression:**
   - "On a scale of 1-10, how would you rate this experience?"
   - "What did you like most?"
   - "What frustrated you the most?"

2. **Design Feedback:**
   - "Does the design feel modern and trustworthy?"
   - "Are the colors too bright/dull?"
   - "Is the text readable?"

3. **Value Proposition:**
   - "After using this, would you recommend CoreIDPin to a friend?"
   - "Would you pay ‚Ç¶900/month for premium features?"
   - "What features would make you upgrade?"

4. **Comparison:**
   - "How does this compare to LinkedIn?"
   - "How does this compare to your current ID verification method?"

5. **Final Question:**
   - "If you could change one thing about CoreIDPin, what would it be?"
